Player Pool 2K19 Entry Form

Welcome to the NCAA Players Pool 2K19. If you've played before, welcome back! If you're new to the pool, thanks for joining. In an effort to streamline the process on my end, this form will be the only way to submit rosters into the pool. Please check back on Selection Sunday!

Rosters are due by noon EST, Thursday, March 21st. Entries entered after noon will not be accepted.

The rules are as follows:
  • The format of the pool is to construct a team of 11 individual players in the NCAA tournament. The team with the most cumulative points at the end of the tournament wins.
  • To start, you'll pick one player from each seed 1-9. I.E. You pick one player from either Virginia (probably), Duke (probably) and whoever gets the final #1 seeds. Repeat for seeds 2 through 9. The final two players come from any teams seeded 10-16, these are your "Wildcards". This is a "set it and forget it" roster; once the tournament starts there are no changes allowed.
  • Wildcard players CANNOT play for the same team.
  • Play-In Games DO NOT count towards a player's total. If you want a player on a Play-In team, you should wait and see if they advance to the field of 64 before submitting your roster.
  • Rosters are due by noon EST, Thursday, March 21st.
  • Entry fee is $40. Payment instructions will be e-mailed to you once your form is submitted. Only digital payments are accepted. We'll likely payout the top 10ish depending on how many entries are received. First place will take home 40%. Other payouts will be determined once I see how many entries are received. I'll send out the breakdown shortly after the tournament starts.
  • 2018's Payouts were as follows:
    • 1st Place - 50%
    • 2nd Place - 15%
    • 3rd Place - 9%
    • 4th Place - 7%
    • 5th Place - 6%
    • 6th Place - 4%
    • 7th Place - 3%
    • 8th Place - 2.5%
    • 9th Place - 2.0%
    • 10th Place - 1.5%
  • You can enter however many rosters you'd like. If you have any issues feel free to e-mail me. Also if you have an edit to a roster, please e-mail me vs. submitting a new form.
  • In the event of a tie, we'll split the prize money between those parties.
  • Once the tournament begins, I will send out a link to the tracker! This is a Google Doc that will update the progress of the pool throughout the tournament. I try to update this very frequently so you can follow the results closely. Last Year's Tracker
